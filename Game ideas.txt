2d graphics

pixel art

protagonist : Adrian Skas

Story:

## Intro

London.

Private detective Adrian Skas sits alone in his apartment.

A knock comes at the door.

A police officer informs him that his wife has been murdered.

The police believe it was a robbery gone wrong.

Adrian doesn't believe them.

He decides to investigate on his own.

---

## Mission 1 - The Black Raven Bar

Objective:

* Travel to the Black Raven Bar
* Ask around for information

Adrian questions several patrons.

Eventually he speaks to the bartender.

Bartender:
"You aren't the first person asking about strange murders."

Adrian:
"What murders?"

Bartender:
"People disappearing. Bodies turning up all over London."

"They say a serial killer is behind it."

Adrian:
"The Silent Reaper?"

Bartender:
"You've heard of him."

Adrian:
"I need information."

Bartender:
"Talk to Frank Ramirez."

"He spends half his life in this bar."

The player walks over to Frank.

Frank:
"What do you want?"

Adrian:
"I'm looking for the Silent Reaper."

Frank:
"I don't know anything."

Adrian:
"The bartender says otherwise."

Frank tries to walk away.

Adrian blocks him.

Frank throws a punch.

The punch accidentally hits another customer.

A massive bar fight breaks out.

---

## Tutorial

Gameplay:

* Learn movement
* Learn melee combat
* Learn dodging
* Learn block

After the fight Adrian corners Frank.

Frank:
"Fine."

"I know some people connected to him."

Adrian:
"Then you're helping me."

Frank:
"Deal."


Frank brings Adrian to a small abandoned warehouse.

Safehouse unlocked.

Features:

* Save game
* Buy upgrades
* Store evidence

Frank explains that several people may have information about the Silent Reaper.

Targets:

* Cole Mercer
* Michael Danilov
* Alisha Jolie

Frank:
"Find them and make them talk."

---

## Mission 2 - Apartment Block One

Objective:

* Find Cole Mercer

Adrian locates Cole.

Cole immediately runs.

Gameplay:

* Foot chase through city streets
* Jumping obstacles
* Dodging traffic

Cole escapes into an abandoned house.

Boss Fight:
Cole Mercer

After the fight Adrian searches the area.

Collectable Evidence:

* Newspaper clipping mentioning recent murders
* Note containing the initials "S.R."

Before dying Cole says:

Cole:
"You don't know what you're doing..."

Adrian:
"What does that mean?"

Cole dies before answering.

Police arrive.

Escape sequence begins.

Adrian becomes wanted by police.

---


Frank:
"Did you get anything?"

Adrian:
"Nothing useful."

Frank:
"Then we move on."


---

## Mission 3 - Apartment Block Two

Objective:

* Locate Michael Danilov

Police patrols are now active around the city.


Adrian and Frank travel together.

Police spot them.

Combat encounter.

During the chaos Frank disappears.

Adrian hears shouting nearby.

He rushes inside a building.

Michael Danilov lies dying.

Frank stands nearby.

Frank:
"He attacked me."

Adrian:
"Did he say anything?"

Frank:
"No."

Michael dies before speaking.

Adrian helps Frank escape.

---


At the safehouse.

The player can examine evidence.

Adrian notices that none of the people they have encountered seemed like killers.

Most appeared frightened.

Frank dismisses it.

Frank:
"People get scared when they're caught."

Adrian accepts the explanation.

---

## Mission 4 - Police Department

Frank arrives with new information.

Frank:
"I heard Alisha Jolie is meeting Detective Ethan Cross."

Adrian:
"Who's Ethan Cross?"

Frank:
"The lead investigator on the Silent Reaper case."

Frank claims he is injured and cannot come.

Objective:

* Infiltrate police headquarters

Gameplay:

* Stealth sections
* Combat encounters

Adrian reaches Ethan Cross's office.

Inside are Ethan and Alisha.

Boss Fight:
Alisha Jolie

Boss Fight:
Ethan Cross

After defeating them, Adrian searches the office.


Objective:

* Search Ethan's office

Adrian discovers a hidden case file.

Inside are reports, witness statements, photographs and investigation notes.

Something doesn't make sense.

Cole Mercer.

Michael Danilov.

Alisha Jolie.

They were not suspects.

They were witnesses.

Witnesses connected to the Silent Reaper investigation.

Adrian turns another page.

The investigation's primary suspect:

Frank Ramirez.

Another page.

A photograph from his wife's murder scene.

Adrian freezes.

Frank's name appears multiple times throughout the investigation.

Everything suddenly fits together.

The witnesses.

The safehouse.

The information.

The lies.

Frank never helped find the killer.

He helped eliminate everyone who could identify him.

---

## Final Mission - The Silent reaper

Objective:

* Return to the safehouse

Adrian arrives.

Frank is gone.

A nearby neighbour points toward the rooftops.

Chase Gameplay:

* Rooftop parkour
* Platforming
* Timed jumps

Frank stays just out of reach.

---


The chase ends on a rooftop overlooking London.

Frank:
"You should have stopped reading."

Adrian:
"You used me."

Frank:
"I gave you what you wanted."

Adrian:
"My wife."

Frank:
"She asked too many questions."

Final Boss Fight:
Frank Ramirez

Multi-phase fight:

* Melee combat
* Rooftop hazards
* Counter attacks

After the battle Frank attempts to escape.

Adrian stops him.

Frank falls from the rooftop.

The Silent Reaper is dead.

---

## Ending

The police arrive.

The case is finally solved.

But Adrian cannot celebrate.

He discovers that he helped murder every witness who could have brought Frank to justice.

The city remembers him as the detective who stopped the Silent Reaper.

Adrian remembers himself as the man who helped him.
